## Daily Routines

### Every Morning (15 minutes)

1. Check PostHog dashboard — any spikes, dropoffs, or errors?
2. Check email — respond to all messages from overnight
3. Check Google Business Profile — any new questions or reviews? Respond within 24h
4. Check Stripe — any payments, refund requests, or disputes?

### Every Evening (10 minutes)

1. Post 1 piece of content on any social platform
2. Check inbox — did Amanda/Dav surface any warm leads today?
3. Note any new customer feedback or common questions → fodder for next blog post

### Weekly (60 minutes — Sundays)

1. Pull KPI report (visitors, leads, customers, revenue)
2. Review week's emails, social, and calls — what got the best response?
3. Plan next week's content, calls, and outreach
4. Update the Google Business Profile with a new post
5. Respond to any pending Google reviews
