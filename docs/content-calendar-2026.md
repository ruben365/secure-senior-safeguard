## Content Production Workflow

1. **Monday AM**: Write and schedule the week's blog post (Notion or Google Docs → publish to site)
2. **Monday PM**: Create 3 social graphics in Canva from blog post key points
3. **Tuesday**: Schedule social posts for the week in Buffer or Later
4. **Wednesday**: Write and send email newsletter (via Resend campaigns)
5. **Thursday–Friday**: Engage with comments, respond to messages, repost user content

**Batch content days** (recommended): Set aside one morning per month to:
- Write 4 blog posts ahead of time
- Create 16+ social graphics in Canva
- Draft 4 email newsletters
- Record 4 short social videos (1 per week)
