# Support SLA — Service Level Agreement

**Organization:** InVision Network  
**Effective Date:** 2026-04-14  
**Version:** 1.0

---

## Overview

InVision Network is committed to providing timely and effective support to all clients and users. This document defines our response time targets, support channels, and escalation path.

---

## Response Time Targets

| Priority | Name     | Description                                                        | First Response | Resolution Target |
|----------|----------|--------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------|-------------------|
| P0       | Critical | Service completely unavailable; data loss or security breach       | 1 hour         | 4 hours           |
| P1       | High     | Core feature broken; significant business impact; security concern | 4 hours        | 24 hours          |
| P2       | Normal   | Feature degraded; moderate impact; general questions               | 24 hours       | 72 hours          |
| P3       | Low      | Minor issue; cosmetic bug; feature request; how-to question        | 72 hours       | Best effort       |

Response times are measured during business hours (Monday–Friday, 9am–5pm Eastern) unless a P0/P1 incident is declared, which triggers 24/7 response.

## Escalation Path

1. **Level 1 — Support Staff**
   - Initial triage and response
   - Handles P2/P3 issues

2. **Level 2 — Technical Team**
   - Escalated from Level 1 when issue requires deeper investigation
   - Handles P1 issues and complex P2 cases

3. **Level 3 — Leadership / Security Team**
   - Escalated P0 and P1 incidents
   - Security breaches and data incidents
   - Contact: security@invisionnetwork.org

---

## Out-of-Scope

This SLA does not cover:
- Issues caused by third-party integrations beyond our control (e.g., Stripe outages, Google OAuth)
- Issues resulting from client misuse or unauthorized modifications
- Force majeure events
